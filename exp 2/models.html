Our Models

Ducati Model 1

  1. Streetfighter V4 is a bike that pushes the concepts of the "Fight Formula" even further
  2. Customize your Streetfighter V4 by making it even sportier: exhaust silencers, mudguards, fins and frame and crankcase protectors in carbon fiber, as well as thermal blankets and stands for those who want to use their Streetfighter V4 on the track.
  3. The evolution follows that of the Panigale V4 family: no fairings, high and wide handlebars, 178 kg weight, 208 hp Desmosedici Stradale engine, biplane wings and latest generation electronic package.
  4. The Streetfighter V4 was an instant success and set new performance benchmarks in the super sports naked segment

Ducati Model 2

  1. The Panigale V4 MY23 represents the last step in the characteristic path of Borgo Panigale's sports bikes.
  2. As is the case with motorcycles engaged in MotoGP and WorldSBK, the Panigale V4 is updated and improved year after year, and the 2022 version represents the most significant evolution since its launch year.
  3. The most significant updates to the 2022 version affect every aspect of the bike: aerodynamics, engine, chassis, ergonomics, and electronics.
  4. The end result is a bike that is even faster on the track and at the same time increasingly intuitive and less fatiguing.

Ducati Model 3

  1. Past, present and future converge in the new Scrambler Ducati 1100 Tribute PRO. Designed to celebrate the history of the air-cooled twin-cylinder engine, 50 years after its introduction to Ducati, the latest addition to the 1100 PRO.
  2. 2021 marks the 50th anniversary of the introduction of the air-cooled twin-cylinder engine on a Ducati, seen for the first time in 1971.
  3. For the occasion, Scrambler Ducati has decided to pay homage to the history of this famous engine by adding a celebratory model to the range: this is how the Scrambler Ducati 1100 Tribute PRO was born.
  4. The new Scrambler Ducati 1100 Tribute PRO celebrates the history of the Borgo Panigale company through the choice of a fascinating “Giallo Ocra” livery, a colour particularly in vogue at the time.
